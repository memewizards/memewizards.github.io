class  PreviewManager
 Handles timeline preview. This manager creates an additional video track on top of the current timeline and renders chunks (small video files of 25 frames) that are added on this track when rendered. This allow us to get a preview with a smooth playback of our project. Only the preview zone is rendered. Once defined, a preview zone shows as a red line below the timeline ruler.
